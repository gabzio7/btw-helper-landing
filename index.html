Questions

Good questions.

ClarityI don't have a fiscal background. Will I understand it?+
That's the whole point. Every rubriek and every field comes with a plain-language explanation — what it means, and why your invoice goes there. Available in Dutch, English and Spanish. You don't need to know the tax code; the AI walks you through it.
ControlDoes BTW Helper file my return for me?+
No — by design. BTW Helper calculates every number and lays it out ready to enter into Mijn Belastingdienst Zakelijk. You paste the figures yourself, under your own DigiD. No third-party authorization, no business verification step — you stay in control of what gets submitted, and it takes about two minutes.
AccuracyHow does it handle foreign invoices and reverse charge?+
Automatically. An invoice from a non-EU supplier with no VAT is converted to euro and self-assessed at 21% — rubriek 4a. EU B2B services map to the right rubriek too. The AI applies the rule and shows you the math for every line, so you can confirm rather than guess.
PrivacyWhat happens to my invoice data?+
Before you mark a quarter as filed, we prompt you to download your full records — your legal 7-year archive, saved to your own drive. After filing, we delete the invoice files from our storage. Your dashboard figures stay, but we don't hold onto your financial documents indefinitely.
ScopeCan I use this instead of full bookkeeping software?+
If you don't need a full ledger — client invoicing, payroll, multi-year accounts — yes. BTW Helper is built for one eenmanszaak that just needs its BTW done right, without the overhead of a full accounting suite. At year-end it also exports everything your accountant needs for your income-tax filing.
TrustHow accurate is the AI, really?+
It's built on a frontier AI model and shows its reasoning on every classification — nothing is a black box. A double-check pass flags anything unusual for your review, and you confirm before anything is filed. The AI does the heavy lifting; the final call is always yours.
CommitmentCan I cancel? Do I get a refund?+
Cancel anytime — you keep access through the end of your current billing period, then your account goes read-only. Your dashboard, history and exports stay available; nothing gets deleted. Monthly plans aren't refunded. Annual plans come with a 14-day money-back window.
