Welcome to BTW Helper (https://btw-helper.nl). By using our service, you agree to these terms.

1. What BTW Helper is

BTW Helper is a VAT filing preparation tool for Dutch eenmanszaak owners. It helps you classify invoices, calculate BTW totals, and prepare your quarterly BTW aangifte. It is not a licensed tax advisor or accountant. You are responsible for reviewing and submitting your own aangifte.

2. Who can use it

BTW Helper is for Dutch sole proprietors (eenmanszaak) with BTW-plicht. You must be 18 or older and provide accurate information when signing up.

3. What we don't do

  • We do not file your BTW aangifte on your behalf
  • We do not provide legally binding tax advice
  • We are not liable for errors in your BTW return caused by incorrect data you provide

4. Free trial and subscriptions

  • New accounts get a 14-day free trial, no credit card required
  • After the trial, a subscription is required to upload invoices and use The Helper
  • Your dashboard and existing data remain visible after the trial
  • If no subscription is started 30 days after trial ends, your account may be deleted
  • Plans are billed monthly or annually via Stripe

5. Cancellation and refunds

  • Cancel anytime via Settings → Manage billing
  • You keep access until the end of your current billing period
  • Monthly plans: no refunds
  • Annual plans: 14-day money-back guarantee from purchase date, contact hello@btw-helper.nl

6. Account deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all data via Settings → Danger zone. Active subscriptions are cancelled automatically on deletion.
